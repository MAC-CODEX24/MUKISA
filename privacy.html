🔒 Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: June 17, 2026  ·  Effective: June 17, 2026

Welcome to MediaHub. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using MediaHub you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data — name, email address, username and password if you create an account.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, clicks, search queries, and referring URLs collected automatically via cookies and server logs.
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and screen resolution.
  • Communications — messages you send through the contact form or by email.
  • Newsletter subscriptions — email address you provide when subscribing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the MediaHub service.
  • Personalise your experience and surface content relevant to your interests.
  • Send newsletters and updates you have opted into (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and deliver relevant advertisements. You can control cookies through your browser settings. For full details see our Cookie Policy.

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (cannot be disabled).
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand traffic patterns (can be opted out).
  • Preference cookies — remember your theme and language settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third parties only:

  • Service providers who operate infrastructure on our behalf (hosting, email delivery, analytics) — bound by data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law.
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger or acquisition, subject to the same privacy commitments.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at theblackhats24@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

MediaHub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of MediaHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
